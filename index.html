Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with Vaultline Trading Systems.

Performance & Verification

How can I verify Vaultline's performance?
  • Vaultline's live trading results are connected to broker data and independently tracked through Myfxbook.
  • You can review performance, drawdown, trades, and account history directly.
Are the results simulated or backtested?
  • The performance presented is based on live broker-connected accounts.
  • Backtests may be used during strategy development, but they are not presented as live results.
What returns should I expect?
  • Returns vary by market conditions, broker, leverage, spreads, commissions, and account configuration.
  • At full allocation, STRATA has historically averaged approximately 6% per month, but returns are never guaranteed.
What drawdown should I expect?
  • Drawdown changes over time and depends on the account setup, broker conditions, and strategy allocation.
  • Vaultline prioritizes controlled risk rather than maximizing returns at any cost.
Has STRATA had losing months?
  • Yes. Every legitimate trading strategy can experience losing months, drawdowns, and slower periods.
Are the displayed returns net of all fees?
  • Broker costs such as spreads, commissions, swaps, and execution differences may affect individual account results.
  • Vaultline fees are disclosed separately before activation.

Capital & Custody

Where does my money sit?
  • Your funds remain in a brokerage account opened in your name.
  • Vaultline does not hold, pool, or custody client capital.
Can Vaultline withdraw my money?
  • No. Vaultline only receives the access required to operate the software and does not receive withdrawal authority.
Do I maintain control of my account?
  • Yes. You retain ownership of the brokerage account and can monitor balances, trades, deposits, withdrawals, and performance.
What is the minimum account size?
  • The minimum depends on the broker, leverage, allocation, and intended risk profile.
  • Your Senior Algorithmic Trading Specialist will recommend the appropriate setup.

Algorithm & Strategy

What is STRATA?
  • STRATA is a diversified basket of automated trading algorithms built to operate across different market conditions and trading environments.
How does STRATA make trading decisions?
  • The system evaluates factors such as price action, market structure, volatility, liquidity, and risk conditions before entering or managing trades.
Is STRATA a single trading bot?
  • No. Multiple strategies operate within the system, each designed for different market conditions.
Is the system fully automated?
  • Trade execution, position management, and risk controls are automated according to predefined strategy logic.
How does STRATA respond to changing markets?
  • Different strategies may become more or less active as market conditions shift, helping reduce dependence on one approach.
Does STRATA use martingale?
  • STRATA does not rely on unlimited position escalation. Position sizing and exposure are controlled through defined risk parameters.
Who manages algorithm updates?
  • Vaultline's quantitative and technical team controls strategy updates, infrastructure, risk settings, and deployment.

Brokers & Execution

Why are different broker options available?
  • Broker availability varies by jurisdiction, leverage, regulation, execution conditions, and platform compatibility.
Can I use a U.S.-regulated broker?
  • Yes, where supported. Lower leverage limits may reduce strategy allocation, expected return, and expected drawdown.
Can I use an internationally regulated broker?
  • Yes, where legally available. International broker options may provide the leverage capacity required for STRATA to operate closer to full allocation.
Why is 200:1 leverage important?
  • STRATA is designed to operate at full allocation in a 200:1 leverage environment.
  • Lower leverage may reduce the amount of exposure the system can deploy.
Will STRATA perform identically at every broker?
  • No. The underlying strategy remains the same, but spreads, commissions, slippage, swaps, liquidity, leverage, and execution speed can affect trading behavior, returns, and drawdown.
Can I change brokers later?
  • Yes, provided the new broker meets Vaultline's technical, regulatory, execution, and platform requirements.

Pricing & Commitment

What does the upfront fee cover?
  • The upfront fee covers software access, system configuration, onboarding, installation, and initial deployment.
What does the monthly fee cover?
  • The monthly fee supports infrastructure, monitoring, maintenance, system access, technical support, and ongoing updates.
Are there performance fees or profit splits?
  • Vaultline does not take a percentage of trading profits unless specifically stated in a separate agreement.
Can I cancel?
  • Cancellation and license terms are clearly outlined in the client agreement before activation.

Support & Onboarding

How long does setup take?
  • Once the brokerage account is approved and funded, guided onboarding typically takes less than 30 minutes.
Do I need trading experience?
  • No. The system is designed to operate automatically, and the onboarding process explains how to monitor your account and access support.
What support is included?
  • Clients receive guided onboarding, technical assistance, ongoing support, and access to the Vaultline community.
How do I get started?
  • Review the verified results, speak with a Senior Algorithmic Trading Specialist, select the appropriate account structure, and complete the guided setup.
